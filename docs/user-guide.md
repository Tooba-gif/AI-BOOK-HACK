# AI Native Textbook for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics - User Guide

## Welcome to the AI Native Textbook

This interactive textbook uses AI to enhance your learning experience in Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This guide will help you navigate and make the most of the platform's features.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar
2. Enter your email address, name, and create a password
3. Verify your email if required
4. You're now ready to start learning!

### Logging In
1. Click on the "Log In" button
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Navigating the Textbook

### Chapters
- All chapters are listed in the left sidebar
- Click on any chapter to begin reading
- Your progress will be automatically saved as you read

### Reading Experience
- Scroll through the content at your own pace
- Your reading progress is tracked automatically
- Interactive elements are embedded throughout the chapters
- Click "Mark 10% More" or "Mark Complete" to manually update progress

## Interactive Features

### AI Chatbot
- Each chapter includes an AI assistant at the bottom
- Type your questions about the content in the chat box
- The AI will provide answers based exclusively on the textbook content
- Answers include citations to specific sections of the book

### Personalization
1. Go to your profile page
2. Set your experience level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
3. Select your field of interest (Robotics, AI, General, etc.)
4. The content will adapt to your background and preferences

### Urdu Translation
- Click the "اردو میں تبدیل کریں" button to switch to Urdu
- Click "Switch to English" to return to English
- Note: Not all chapters may have Urdu translations available

## Tracking Your Progress

### Chapter Progress
- A progress bar at the top of each chapter shows your completion percentage
- Your progress is saved automatically as you read
- View your overall progress in the "Dashboard" section

### Learning Resources
- Each chapter includes automatically generated summaries, quizzes, and learning boosters
- These resources help reinforce key concepts from the chapter
